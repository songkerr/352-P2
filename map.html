Polhill Reserve Loop

Rating:

The Polhill Reserve loop is a 7.3km walk, taking about 2 hours

This loop walk has a common entry and exit point and is suitable for those with average fitness.

Getting there:

There are two buses you can take to reach the start of the walk.
Take the number 9 bus from the city to the start of the walk on Aro Street. Or jump on the number 20 bus to Highbury to walk part of the loop downhill.
